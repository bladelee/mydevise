Billing

Billing Date

DigitalOcean bills on the 1st of each month for the prior month's usage. That means you will receive an invoice on March 1st for your usage for Feb 1 - Feb 28th. After the invoice is generated we bill for the outstanding balance either on the 1st or 2nd of each month.

Balance

The balance displays how much is currently outstanding on your account. If there is a sum owed it will appear in red. If your current credits offset your current usage you will see the remaining balance of your credits in green.
The balance is updated daily.

Payment - Credit Card or Paypal

DigitalOcean accepts Credit Cards or Paypal payments. If you provide a credit card we will automatically attempt to bill you on the 1st of each month for the prior month's usage. If the payment fails we will automatically attempt to re-bill you until the payment clears once a day.

Current Usage

Current Usage displays the current charges that you have incurred for the current month. You can also click on the link to see an itemized invoice showing the specific charges that your account has incurred for the month.
